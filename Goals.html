Short & Long Term Goals

On this page we have set our short and long term goals. These goals will need to be accomplished for us to succeed

Short Term Goals

  • Have in-person meetings
  • Be there to support each other through the highs and lows
  • Complete our group assignments ahead of time and on schedule
  • Learn from each other's strengths and weaknesses
  • Encourage each other to always do our best

Long Term Goals

  • Make it to final round in case competition
  • Stay in touch after graduation
  • Become a leader within the organization and workplace
  • Create a fun environment to get to know each other outside of school
  • Succeed and grow through the challenges
